Our Team

The Oxford Wildcat Robotics Team 5291 has 6th, 7th and 8th graders spread out into 15 people, with three returning team members and twelve rookies. This year TOWR 5291 had 45 students apply so we started two new teams: 11230 and 11231. We are very glad that Oxford has more teams this year and are looking forward to the competitions.
